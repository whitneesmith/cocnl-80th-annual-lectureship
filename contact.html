Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is included in my registration?

Registration includes access to all lectures, workshops, Bible studies, continental breakfast daily, fellowship meals, and all conference materials. Hotel accommodation is separate.

Can I register at the door?

Yes, walk-in registration is available, but pre-registration is strongly encouraged to guarantee materials, seating, and meal planning. Walk-in rates may be higher than advance registration.

What should I wear?

Business casual or church attire is appropriate for all sessions. Comfortable walking shoes are recommended as you'll be moving between different rooms and floors.

Are meals provided?

Continental breakfast is included daily with registration. Some fellowship meals are also included. Lunch is typically on your own with many nearby restaurant options.

What about accessibility needs?

The hotel is fully ADA compliant with accessible rooms and facilities. Please contact us in advance for any special accommodations, hearing assistance, or dietary restrictions.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Registration refunds are available until February 15, 2026, minus a $25 processing fee. Hotel reservations are non-refundable per hotel policy. Travel insurance is recommended.

Is there childcare available?

Limited childcare may be available for certain sessions. Please contact us in advance to discuss your needs and availability. Additional fees may apply.

How do I register a group?

Groups of 10 or more may qualify for discounted rates. Contact us directly to discuss group registration options and special pricing.
