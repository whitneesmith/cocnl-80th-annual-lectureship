Featured Speakers

Inspiring messages from renowned Bible teachers and ministers

Keynote Speaker

Dr. John Smith Photo

Dr. John Smith

Senior Minister, First Church of Christ, Nashville, TN

Dr. Smith has been preaching the Gospel for over 35 years and is widely recognized as one of the most influential Bible teachers in the Churches of Christ. He holds a Ph.D. in Biblical Studies from Vanderbilt University and has authored twelve books on Christian living and biblical interpretation.

His keynote address, "Here We Stand: Foundation of Faith," will explore the unchanging principles that have guided the church for centuries and how they apply to our modern challenges.

Speaking Sessions:

  • • Sunday Evening: Opening Address
  • • Tuesday Evening: "Standing Together in Unity"
  • • Thursday Morning: "Standing Strong in Truth"
